Sloppy Joes

What Is a Sloppy Joe?

A Sloppy Joe is a classic American sandwich made with seasoned ground beef and onions simmered in a tangy, tomato-based sauce, all piled onto a soft hamburger bun.

The dish is believed to have originated in the 1930s in Sioux City, Iowa, where a cook named Joe is said to have created the original “loose meat sandwich.”

How to Make Sloppy Joes

The full recipe is just below, but here’s a quick look at the simple steps involved in making this timeless favorite:

  1. Sauté the ground beef with onions and green peppers until browned.
  2. Drain any excess fat, then mix in the rest of the ingredients.
  3. Let it simmer for around 30 minutes. Spoon the mixture onto hamburger buns and serve warm.
