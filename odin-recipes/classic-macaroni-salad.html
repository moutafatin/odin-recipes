Classic Macaroni Salad

This creamy macaroni salad with celery, onion, green pepper, carrot, and pimentos is easy to make and is the perfect crowd-pleasing dish to bring to your next picnic or potluck.

Macaroni Salad Ingredients

Here’s what you’ll need to prepare this classic macaroni salad:

  • Macaroni: The star of the dish — macaroni pasta is essential.
  • Condiments: A rich, creamy mix of mayonnaise and yellow mustard gives the salad its signature flavor.
  • Sugar and Vinegar: White sugar brings a hint of sweetness, perfectly balanced by the tang of white vinegar. Feel free to adjust the sugar to match your taste.
  • Seasonings: Simply seasoned with salt and black pepper to enhance the other flavors.
  • Vegetables: You'll need celery, onion, and bell pepper. Grated carrots and pimento peppers are optional but add great color and extra taste.

How to Make Macaroni Salad

Here’s a quick step-by-step guide to making your own macaroni salad:

  1. Cook the macaroni in salted boiling water until tender.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the mayonnaise, mustard, vinegar, sugar, salt, and pepper.
  3. Add the cooked macaroni and chopped vegetables, then stir until everything is evenly coated.
  4. Refrigerate until chilled — it tastes best cold!
